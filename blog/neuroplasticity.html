Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.


Knowing that we have the ability to positively or negatively affect how our brain develops. Our brains continue to work at a high level for extended periods of time the more we challenge and engage our mind and body. The development of neuroplastic change has a wide range of potential benefits including the ability to pick up new skills, improve current cognitive abilities and capacities, it also aids in the rehabilitation of people that have had strokes and many more .
